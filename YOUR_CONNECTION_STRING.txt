═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════
  WHY @ IS ENCODED AS %40:
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════

In URLs, special characters have special meanings:
- @ separates username:password from host
- So if your password contains @, it must be encoded as %40
- Otherwise MongoDB thinks the @ is part of the URL structure

Your password: Akshat@cmrit
Encoded:      Akshat%40cmrit
